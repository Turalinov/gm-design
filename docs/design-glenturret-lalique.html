The Glenturret Lalique Restaurant

Back
The Lalique hotel and restaurant at Château Lafaurie-Peyraguey opened its doors in June 2018. This luxury Relais& Châteaux establishment is a haven of peace set in the vineyards of Sauternes in the greatest terroir of Crus Classés. From the first light of dawn to the last glow of sunset, an atmosphere of discreet and subdued elegance reigns within its walls, suitable for private gatherings and lavish receptions alike. French savoir-faire and savoir-vivre are a subtle and all-pervading presence (Text: Isabelle Bunisset).
