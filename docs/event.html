David Yarrow

Back 24-30 December 2023 Previous
David Yarrow's evocative and immersive photography of life on earth is most distinctive.

Scottish photographer David Yarrow is a pioneer of fine art photography, known for visiting isolated locations and communities to capture the sublime beauty of the natural world.

His dramatic and highly evocative images, usually black and white, fuse the knowledgeable approach of David Attenborough with Ridley Scott’s visual drama and the exquisite composition of Ansel Adams.

His photographs fetch high prices across international auction houses, making Yarrow one of the most coveted photographers alive today.

He has equally shown an unchanging commitment to charitable causes and protecting wildlife through fundraising for various NGOs and the British NHS.
