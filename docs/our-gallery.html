G&M Design Gallery

We have been honoured to exhibit works by some of the greatest artists of modern times. Pablo Picasso, Damien Hirst, Yves Klein, are just some of the legendary names to have graced our walls.
Available artworks

Opened in 2018 in Monaco, G&M Design Gallery is a new, contemporary art gallery, specialising in fine art, contemporary art, and photography, from all over the world.

We work with a wide and diverse array of the world’s finest living photographers and artists, including David Yarrow, David Bailey, Mariano Vivanco, Tyler Shields, and Mark Evans to name just a few.

We offer you the chance to own an exceptional range of artworks, any of which would be the highlight of your art collection / any of which would enhance your art collection.

Since our inception, we’ve put on more than 25 successful exhibitions, each more ambitious than the last. As our ambitions grow, so do we. And we grow together with our art projects every day. We feel incredibly lucky to have worked with some of the biggest and most talented names in contemporary art.

At G&M Design Gallery, the pictures themselves are just a small part of what we offer. Our hugely renowned  interior design department can take the taste of each individual client and help you choose the perfect piece of art for your home, office, or any other interior. We can also provide full conceptual projects combining several works in harmony, ideal for bigger spaces.
